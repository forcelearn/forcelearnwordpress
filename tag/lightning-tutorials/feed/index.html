Custom domain is which helps us to make custom url to login and look and feel like it your own branded URL. By using custom domain you can help users to login into salesforce from social media like facebook, twitter,linkedin ..so on.

Steps to create custom domain

  • In order to setup custom domain you need to goÂ  Path :Â  setup- domain(in quick search)- MyDomain.

  • If you find you domain sucessfully click on register domain and wait for couple of minutes.

  • you will receive mail for salesforce login to your domain and then deploy to all users
  • Cheers !! you are allowed to create lightning components in salesforce lighting
