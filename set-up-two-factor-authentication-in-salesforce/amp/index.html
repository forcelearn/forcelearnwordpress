Set Up Two-Factor Authentication in Salesforce

Two-Factor Authentication is helps you secure more our authentication based on user login.Two-Factor Authentication contains two things or two factor required to access your account. The first factor is something your knows like your username and password and second factor is Something you have like connected mobile device which show secure code need to matched to successfully login to your account.
Two-Factor Authentication is also known as 2FA, two step verification or TFA.
